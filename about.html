About Me

I am recent Computer Science graduate from Fordham University— graduated May 18th, 2024. With a 3.9 cumulative GPA, I prioritized my studies while also pursuing career and personal development.

I'm from Winthrop, Massachusetts, a small suburb near Boston, where I graduated from Winthrop High School.

At Fordham University, I've pursued my interests in Computer Science, taking courses like Computer Science I & II, Discrete Structures, Data Structures, and more. I've also explored my passion for Visual Arts with classes in Graphic Design, Photography, and Visual Thinking.

Beyond classes, I've grown through various roles, including tutoring Computer Science basics and C++ principles at Fordham's Higher Education Opportunity Program (HEOP). I'm involved in Girls Who Code, Computer Science Society, Women in STEM, and SPIRE—a women's empowerment club.

In 2023, I worked at a startup called toTree, enhancing my skills in web development with Java and JavaScript. I also learned new tools like Firebase and Google Ads for web development and data analytics.

Besides my studies and career goals, I'm a proud Celtics fan (excited about the 2024 National Championship win!) and enjoy expressing my creativity through scrapbooking, painting, and drawing.

Go Rams!
